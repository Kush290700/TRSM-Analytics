KPI deep dive

Commercial scale, demand, pricing, and finance command center

Secondary KPIs are grouped by decision context so leaders can separate scale, demand, pricing quality, and finance posture without reading a flat card wall.
Finance and margin
Revenue, cost, profit, and margin anchor the command center with the protected finance view leadership expects first.
Commercial scale
Orders, units, and shipped weight show how large the business is under the active filters and whether volume is holding.
Customer demand
Customer breadth and order activity explain whether movement is broad-based demand or concentrated account behavior.
Pricing and basket quality
Basket size, realized pricing, and unit economics show whether growth is quality growth rather than just more activity.
