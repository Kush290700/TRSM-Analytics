KPI deep dive

Operating scale, demand quality, pricing, and trust

Top-line finance stays in the executive scorecard above. This layer narrows the view to the supporting KPIs leaders use to judge scale, customer quality, monetization, and data confidence.
Operational scale
Orders, units, and shipped weight confirm how large the current filtered business actually is.
Demand and customer movement
Customer breadth, revenue per account, and movement shares show whether the window is broad-based or concentrated in a few relationships.
Pricing and basket quality
Basket size, realized price, and unit economics show whether growth is quality growth rather than just more activity.
Trust and governance
Coverage, mapping, and governed refresh signals stay visible so leadership knows how much confidence to place in the view.
